About Me

I am Vaibhavi Gadre, a counseling psychologist and certified trauma-informed yoga teacher. My passion lies in making the teachings of yoga more accessible and relevant in the current mental health space.

With extensive experience in both therapeutic counseling and yoga instruction, I offer a unique blend of mental health support and physical wellbeing practices. My approach integrates evidence-based psychological techniques with the ancient wisdom of yoga philosophy.

I believe in empowering individuals to reconnect with their inner strength and find balance in their lives. Through my sessions, workshops, and classes, I create a safe and nurturing space for growth, healing, and transformation.
